Dashboard

Five analytical views

Tab 01

Project Overview

Research context, objectives, and methodology summary. Entry point for understanding the analytical framework behind the dashboard.

Tab 02

Death Rates Choropleth

Country-level mortality map with a light-blue to dark-red gradient. Darker shading indicates higher deaths per million.

Tab 03

Vaccination vs. Mortality

Scatter plot with regression overlay and continent filter. Point size encodes total death count, revealing the inverse relationship.

Tab 04

Continental Analysis

Side-by-side box plots comparing vaccination coverage and mortality distributions across all continents.

Tab 05

Vaccination Distribution

Overlapping histograms comparing vaccination rates for high vs. low mortality countries with an adjustable mortality threshold slider.

Data Sources

death_table.csv

COVID-19 pandemic death rates by country — deaths per million, total deaths, and confirmed case counts.

vaccine_percent.csv

COVID-19 vaccination percentages by location — number vaccinated and percent of population covered.

rnaturalearth (R package)

Natural Earth geographic data providing country boundary polygons, ISO codes, and continent assignments for choropleth rendering.
